Questions

Before you start

What does the free trial include?

Three free searches, no card required. Enough to put a real task in front of Gideon and see the quality before you decide.

What is the difference between the tiers?

Simple Access gives you Gideon for everyday business and advisory work with 100 queries a month. Professional adds 1,000 queries, priority handling and extended memory for heavier, multi-step work.

Is my data secure?

Access is tiered and you can bring your own provider keys. Enterprise adds private deployment and keeps your data under your own infrastructure.

What is the Meeting Room?

A shared workspace where specialist agents work the same problem together and hand work between them in real time, rather than one model answering alone. It is live and in active use now.

Can Gideon replace my team?

No. Gideon does the reading, analysis and drafting at speed so your people spend their time on judgement and relationships. Treat its output as a strong first draft to review.
